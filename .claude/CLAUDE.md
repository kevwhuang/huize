# Huize

Corporate website for 惠泽健康 (Huize Health), a Shanghai-based digital health technology company providing elderly care, rehabilitation, nursing, health management, and insurance services across 500 cities in China. Features homepage with animated stats, service tabs, news cards, network accordions, and contact form, plus placeholder subpages. Built with Astro, React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, and GSAP. Deployed on Netlify with Supabase.
